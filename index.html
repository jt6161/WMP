Collaborative Film Group

This is a web page about the bad ass comedic sketch writing group called "Where's My Pants?!". The group consists of mostly women that have had their short films screened in Phoenix Arizona. The group had a short film screened at "A Night of Misfit Films" a film festival at the SIC Sense Sketch Comedy Troupe theater at the end of 2016. Most recently the group particpated in the 2017 IFP-Phoenix Beat the Clock challenge with their short film "Foxy Force 5". The group was awarded "Best Art Direction" for the film.
